You've probably heard AI is going to change everything for small business. Maybe you've even tried ChatGPT a few times.

But here's what nobody tells you: the gap between "I've heard of this" and "I'm actually using this to save time" is enormous — and most resources assume you have a tech team to help you cross it.

That's exactly why Better Stacks exists.

## What we do here

Every week I publish one AI workflow, explained in plain English, for real small business owners. No jargon, no tech prerequisites, no 47-step setup process.

Just: here's the problem, here's the tool, here's exactly what to do.

## Who this is for

You run a business. You're good at what you do. You don't have hours to spend figuring out software — you have a business to run.

If that's you, you're in the right place.
